13.‎ ‎ ‎ Cinema

by Lee Know & Seungmin


This is a song na kinanta ng dalawa kong bias sa stray kids, si lee know and seungmin hehe. One of my favorite songs ko siya ngayon ng stray kids, and nung nakita ko yung english translation, pwede ko rin pala maging song ito para sayo. Every time I feel sad, di ka siguro aware pero you’re always there to make me smile again, “In the blackout, a door opened”. In a room I thought na palagi ako magiging malungkot dahil I feel like a loner sa room HAHA (it feels like I’m forcing others na kausapin ako but I’m still thankful), you came and a story starts to rise up. A story na hindi ko ineexpect na aabot sa point na you will become one of the important person in my life. Parang cinema nga yung story natin eh, maraming nanonood, marami rin yung nag susupport, may fanclub pa nga kaming ginawa HAHA, pero sana natuwa ka nung nagpapicture kami kasama ka kasi mga ventina supporters talaga yan sila na nag-abang sa confession ko sayo. Sa outro ng kanta (try mo imove sa bandang huli ng audio hehe), it shows kung paano na both yung names natin nasa ending credits, I’m hoping na sa totoong buhay talagang together na nasa ending credits yung names natin eh HAHA, sana gets 😅, and I hope you’ll always remember “When you cannot find your way, I’ll be waiting”, palagi kita hihintayin ano man ang mangyari na kahit hindi mo na mahanap yung way mo I’ll try my best to stay as always.
