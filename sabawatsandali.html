02.‎ ‎ ‎ Sa Bawat Sandali

by Amiel Sol


This song naman is one of my comfort songs right now, which I always remember God, my family, friends, and ikaw of course, every time na pinapakinggan ko. “Kapag magulo na ang mundo, ikaw ang payapang hinahanap ko”, palagi ko naalala kung paano ko palagi kinakausap si Lord every time it feels heavy and I felt helpless, but thankfully he gave me my family, friends, and ikaw na maovercome yung mga lungkot na nafefeel ko sa araw-araw, which I’m very thankful of. Sa tuwing nakakausap kita na kahit maikli lang, nalulungkot nga ako minsan kasi bigla ka mawawala pero gets ko naman kasi hindi naman palagi dapat kitang kausap, but thank you for always making time na makausap ako na kahit hanggang chat pa lang, nafefeel ko yung efforts na binibigay mo para sa akin:D. Kapag nakakausap kita, gumagaan palagi yung loob ko, nawawala yung pagod ko from schoolworks, and tulad noong nakakausap kita by congratulating lang, same na same sila ng saya na nararamdamn ko until now. Tulad ng lyrics na “Tumakbo ka rin patungo sa ‘kin, kapag bumibigat na ang iyong dibdib”, feel free to talk to me and open up din sakin ng nagpapalungkot sayo if you’re comfy, I’m always ready to listen even though di talaga ako marunong magcomfort ng iba, but I’ll try. Anyways, thank you for always making me happy even for the smallest things.
